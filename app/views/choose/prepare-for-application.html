Research Insights

Many users stated that they had questions and concerns that they would like answered but found it difficult to get in contact with what they thought would be the relevant organisation

Evidence:

User research

UKVI services on GOV.UK

Researchers: Clare Ridd and Joe Coker
Participants: 12 interviews with visit visa holders
Date: October 2017

Some tried to contact the VAC centres or UKVI by phone to get advice

  • "I remember struggling to be able to get any, if I had any questions or any concerns, not being able to have anyone to get through to" (R6 - P10)
  • "We try our best to contact Visa UK for some help, but it was impossible, when I visited the website" (R9 - P3)
  • "UK visa application centre in my country in my city. They helped advise me to put as much as possible. But for that I needed to come there personally. They didn't have any mobile phone or email address. That was another point which was very painful for me during the application" (R6-12)
  • "I think if there was a possibility of having a telephone helpline that is central that works, that would be really good" (R6 - P10)
  • "Open only from this time to this time and then there's a time difference to make the call" (R6 - P7)

Users who managed to get in contact with UKVI by phone felt the call charges were excessive.

Evidence:

User research

UKVI services on GOV.UK

Researchers: Clare Ridd and Joe Coker
Participants: 12 interviews with visit visa holders
Date: October 2017

  • "when we had doubts about a question or two we tried to call the support centre, the UK support centre or something like that, but it costs about £5 or something like that for questions to be answered. I don't think that is very…that's not the nicest thing" (R6 - P7)
  • "also it's already an international call, and then spending £6 or £10 for a minute I don't think that's very fair… They either deducted £5 or something, but it's very expensive to do that. I think it should have a free help centre or something like that" (R6 - P7)

While others were had been unable to reach anyone expressed they would be willing to pay extra to speak with someone

  • "But even if there was a bit added to the fee just to be able to cover costs of people being able to make a phone call, or to be able to check on progress" (R6-P10)
