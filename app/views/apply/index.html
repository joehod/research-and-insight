Research Insights

Users became annoyed when they had to repeat all the same steps for each visa application to the UK. They were expecting the information they had entered previously to be saved and reused.

  • Backup evidence 1
  • Backup evidence 2
  • Backup evidence 4

Providing travel history is difficult and anxiety-inducing beacuse users who travel a lot worry that forgetting to enter a trip will mean their application is rejected because they have not told the truth.

User research

UKVI services on GOV.UK

Researchers: Clare Ridd and Joe Coker
Participants: 12 interviews with visit visa holders
Date: October 2017

Users also became anxious because there is not enough space to enter in their full travel history so they have to choose which trips to include. They worry because they do not know which trips they should incude.

  • "Because I get so many visas…I have to dig up two passports, look through the stamps… that's really difficult…suddenly I'll be in the shower and I'll think 'oh my God what about that trip? I forgot that one.' [R6-P12]
  • "You'll have a random trip 5 years ago… it just slips your memory"; [R6-P12]
  • "I probably do 10 trips a year… they ask back 5/10 years…passport has changed…I have to dig up all the passports, look through the stamps"; "but the info isn't always in the passport… not all countries do it in the same way" [R6-P12]
  • "If there was a country where I had multiple trips per year, I only put in the first, then in notes section I said that"; [R6-P12]
  • "Details of all the countries that you have visited…that's a bit over the top"; [R6-P12]
  • "They need information on previous travels separated by commonwealth and non-Commonwealth countries"; [R6-P12]
  • "I'm not sure for the rationale for some questions… previous passports… details of the countries you've visited"; [R6-P12]
  • "So being from originally from Kazakhstan, and now living in Europe. This was not a simple process, there was a lot of travelling before this in my life. So I was pretty sure that I shouldn't put all the information there. I tried to put the most relevant or the latest ones and I just hoped it would be fine, it would be enough. But again in the application there was no guidance on how many trips I should put in or how much information should be there" [R6-P12]
