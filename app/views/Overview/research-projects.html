Overview

Research projects

Ongoing:

The Visas (Access UK) project aims to digitise all paper based Visa forms and to provide a single online application process for all Visa and immigration services:

  • Accessed via GOV.UK, for all in and out of country applications
  • Replaces all of today's online and paper-based application processes
  • Fewer, more intuitive questions ordered based on customer feedback
  • Used to apply, pay and book an appointment
  • A single online application form for dependants and groups

    • Researchers: Bhavik Morar, Andras Dombi

Digital permissions is a programme encompassing 2 goals;

  • 1. Replacement of existing printed visas with an electronic visa
  • 2. The introduction of an ETA (electronic travel authority)

    • The primary focus has been investigating and learning about electronic visas as a stepping stone to ETA. The team has not yet developed, nor has the panel assessed, the core of the new IT systems to support the ETA.

    Researchers: Amy Everett

The Satellite Tracking service allows Immigration Enforcement to locate people who have a tag. These people are currently Foreign National Offenders who are not detained and are waiting to be sent back to their country.

Researchers: Jane Devine
